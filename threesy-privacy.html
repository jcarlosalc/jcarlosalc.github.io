Privacy Policy

Threesy! — Last updated: March 27, 2026

Threesy! ("the App") is developed and operated by Almond Party Labs Corporation.

Data We Collect

The App stores game progress locally on your device, including your score, best score, settings, and purchased items. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

The App displays ads provided by AppLovin MAX. AppLovin may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for the purpose of serving relevant ads. You will be asked for your permission before any tracking identifier is used. You can manage your preferences at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.

For more information on AppLovin's privacy practices, visit applovin.com/privacy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Purchase history is managed by Apple.

Third-Party Services

Other than AppLovin MAX for advertising, the App does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
